Prosciutto Pizza

Theres been a lot of times in my life where I can't really think of what to have for dinner. So to combat this issue, I always fall back onto this simple recipe.

*note* These ingredients are quite flexible, feel free to add more toppings if you desire.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base (can be store bought or homemade)
  • Pizza sauce (again store bought or homemade)
  • Prosciutto
  • 2 Mushrooms
  • Handful of olives
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. First preheat oven to 200 degrees or 180 fan force.
  2. Put a few dollops of pizza sauce on the pizza base and spread with a spoon, until you have an even coverage of the base
  3. Dice mushrooms and olives, I like to cut the mushrooms into matchstick shapes.
  4. Tear prosciutto with hands and place evenly around pizza base.
  5. Spread mushrooms and olives around pizza base.
  6. Take small spoonfuls of ricotta cheese and evenly distribute around pizza base.
  7. Cover pizza in mozzarella cheese, get a nice even coverage but not too thick.
  8. Finally add a few leaves of rocket or your preferred leafy green on top.
  9. Place in middle of the oven under a baking sheet. Cook for 10-15 mins or until cheese is golden brown and base is cooked through.
  10. Remove from oven, cut into quarters and enjoy
