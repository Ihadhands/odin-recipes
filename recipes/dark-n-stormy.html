Dark and Stormy Cocktail

Picture this, you've just gotten home from a long day of work, your brain is mush, you're tired as hell and you just want to relax. Well this is the perfect cocktail to completely numb you out to the world and relax.

*note sinking a few of these will definitely put you to sleep

Ingredients

  • One part spiced rum (ideally kraken or something dark).
  • Two parts alcoholic ginger beer (something not too sweet).
  • Couple ice cubes.
  • 2 quarters of a fresh lime.
  • Fresh Mint for garnish.

Steps

  1. First place a few ice cubes into a short glass.
  2. Mix one part rum and 2 parts ginger beer into glass.
  3. Squeeze lime quarters into glass and then drop both in.
  4. Garnish glass with mint.
  5. Knock it back with a good album or your favorite movie.
