Grilled Ham and Cheese

There is nothing better in this world than a delicious grilled ham and cheese.

So without further ado, this is my easy grilled cheese. Something that will satisfy anybody.

Ingredients

  • 20g Ham off the bone.
  • Mozzarella cheese.
  • 2 slices of your favorite bread.
  • Butter.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Butter the outer sides of your bread, optional you can butter the inside as well.
  2. Place ham on one of the inner sides of the bread.
  3. Apply a little salt and pepper.
  4. Place a handful of mozzarella on top of the ham, honestly as much as you want.
  5. Put the other side of the bread on top.
  6. Place a nonstick fry pan onto the stove on medium heat (or if you are like me and are lazy use a sandwich press).
  7. Place the sandwich carefully onto the fry pan and let it sizzle.
  8. Flip when the bottom side is nice and golden and continue to cook until the top side is golden brown and cheese has melted.
  9. Remove from the pan, cut into halves and enjoy immediately (hot tip, try to avoid burning the roof of your mouth).
